What this dashboard pulls

  • Sound level: USGS instantaneous gage height for Currituck Sound on east bank at Corolla.
  • Ocean data: NOAA NDBC Duck FRF realtime and spectral feeds, plus northern/offshore buoy water-temperature stations.
  • Atlantic tides: NOAA CO-OPS high/low predictions from Duck Pier, the closest verified Atlantic tide station for Carova.
  • Local weather: Currituck WeatherSTEM Carova fire station public portal, with NWS point forecast fallback.
